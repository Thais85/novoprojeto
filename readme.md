### How to order a pizza on Ifood

* Open Ifood.
* Tap the search icon.
* Write "pizza" on the search bar.
* Choose your favorite pizza place.
* Choose your favorite flavor.
* Choose the size according to your hunger.
* Enjoy your pizza!
